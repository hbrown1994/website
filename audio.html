Audio Engineering

Since 2014, I have been active as a freelance audio engineer in recording, live sound, post-production, and audio restoration contexts. Currently, I also work as the Technical Director and in-house audio engineer for Ensemble Dal Niente and as the lead Front of House engineer at the High Desert Soundings Festival. Previously, I was employed as a recording and live sound engineer at the Oberlin Conservatory of Music's Audio Services department and as an Assistant Audio Engineer at the Marlboro Music Festival.

My years of musical training as a composer, performer, and music technologist, combined with my technical audio engineering knowledge, make me well-suited for working on projects involving complex contemporary scores, non-traditional instrumentations, live electronics, multi-channel speaker systems, and other idiosyncratic performance configurations found in new music, experimental music, improvised music, and electroacoustic music.

Below are some of the services I offer. Email me for rates.

If you needs are not expressed in this list, send me an email and I will happy to speak with you about how I can help you achieve your sonic goals.
